About the Institute

Sincere teaching, not just completed lessons.

Hidayah Hub Institute began with a simple conviction: that learning to read and recite the Qur'an correctly should be personal, patient and taught by someone who is genuinely invested in the student's progress — not a recorded lecture watched alone.

Every class is live. Every student is followed up. Every lesson opens with a revision of the last, so nothing is learned and forgotten.

Inside a Class

How each session runs

  1. 01

    Revision

    We begin by reviewing what was taught in the previous class.

  2. 02

    New lesson

    The next portion is taught live, with explanation in your chosen language.

  3. 03

    Practice & questions

    You read aloud, get corrected in real time, and ask anything unclear.

  4. 04

    Homework set

    A short task is assigned to practise before the next class.

  5. 05

    Notes shared

    Attendance is logged and notes or PDFs are sent after class.

  6. 06

    Follow-up

    If you miss a class, we reach out — you're not left behind quietly.
