About the Institute

Sincere teaching, not just completed lessons.

Hidayah Hub Institute began with a simple conviction: that learning to read and recite the Qur'an correctly should be personal, patient and taught by someone who is genuinely invested in the student's progress — not a recorded lecture watched alone.

Every class is live. Every student is followed up. Every lesson opens with a revision of the last, so nothing is learned and forgotten.

"We don't simply teach the Qur'an. We help learners build a lifelong relationship with it — through authentic guidance, structured learning, and consistent practice."

From the Founder

I started this institute because too many students complete lesson after lesson without ever truly understanding — or retaining — what they've read. Every class here is taught by me, personally.

Why Students Stay

What learning here actually looks like

◆

Live, not recorded

Every class happens in real time, with room for questions and correction as you read.

◆

One-to-one or group

Learn individually at your own pace, or alongside peers in a small group setting.

◆

Taught in your language

Instruction available in English, Hindi, Urdu, and basic Arabic — whichever you're most comfortable in.

◆

Revision, always

Notes & homework

If you miss a class

Our Framework

  1. 01
